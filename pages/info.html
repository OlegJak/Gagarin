About Us

The Center for Creative Development and Leisure "GAGARIN keskus" was created to organize creative, developing and interesting leisure activities for city residents.

The center is located at: st. Pushkina 5, Narva, Estonia.

History

The Center for Creative Development and Leisure "Gagarin" was established in 2005 with the aim of developing the creativity of various categories of the population. During this time, we have grown from one-time projects and ideas into a permanent needlework center, which hosts numerous classes, courses, seminars and master classes.

The Gagarin Center organizes events both on its own initiative and at the request of various educational and other institutions. Our target audience is, first of all, adults, for whom there are practically no opportunities for organized creative pastime.

In 2007, our center received an award from the Integration Fund for the best idea of the project for adults "Masters of my Motherland".

In 2012, our organization was included in the collection “Successful Public Organizations of Ida-Virumaa”.

Since 2013, we have been included in the list of NGOs operating in the public interest, with income tax exemption.

In 2019, we were awarded for the best use of Ida-Virumaa Local Initiative Program (LIP) funds for the benefit of society.

In 2020, the Estonian Association of Adult Educators ANDRAS and the Ministry of Education recognized us as the “Employee of the Year in Adult Education in Ida-Virumaa”.

Our teachers and masters

Various activities and training activities are conducted under the guidance of experienced and qualified teachers. Teachers conducting training sessions at the Center must have pedagogical experience and professional knowledge of the technologies taught. These are either professional masters with teaching experience, or teachers who have been trained in various technologies.

Learning environment

All of the Center's classrooms are licensed for use as classrooms or training workshops. All classes are equipped with comfortable workplaces, professional certified educational equipment, ventilation, general and local lighting, electrical audit of the power supply system. There are two bathrooms, washbasins, necessary personal hygiene products, a kitchen with utensils and equipment.

The premises of the center are adapted for teaching people with special needs (ramp, electric elevator, toilet, etc.).
