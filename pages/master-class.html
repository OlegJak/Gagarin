Teaching stained glass Tiffany

From September 1, classes on Tiffany's stained glass windows began in adult circles. Classes are held once a week for 3.5 hours. The first group works on Sundays from 10-00 to 13-45, the second group on Wednesdays from 17-30 to 21-00. You can join classes at any time.

The cost is 50 euros per month (Sunday group + 2 euros for a coffee break), all materials and tools for work are included in the price.

You can connect at any time, but be sure to contact us in advance to arrange availability.

Fusing master classes in February

On February 24, master classes in fusing for adults and teenagers from 16 years old will be held.

Participation conditions are traditional, as are the registration conditions (last name, first name, phone number and selected group - send to info@gagarin.ee).

You will have the opportunity to do any work that you have in mind. An album with possible works will also be presented on the site. As part of the master class, a practical lesson will take place, which consists in creating glass compositions for their subsequent sintering in a special furnace. Finished works, depending on their total number, will be finalized and issued as they are ready (1-4 days).

Participation fee - 12€
For children - 6€

Wicker wreath - master class

At the master class, you will learn how to weave an elegant spiral base for a wreath, which you can decorate immediately here or later on your own. Weaving is made from paper tubes twisted from newspaper sheets, i.e. the material is as accessible as possible, but the technique and techniques are completely similar to weaving from a vine.

The tubes for work will be provided ready-made, but the principle of twisting will be demonstrated to those who wish. The made wreath is well covered with any paint, but even in its original form it has a very stylish look. The size of the wreath at the choice of the participant of the master class.

Participation conditions are traditional, as are the registration conditions (last name, first name, phone number and selected group - send to info@gagarin.ee).

Participation fee - 15€
